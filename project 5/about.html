About Us


TechRevival is a company founded in 2019. Whenever a friend's phone would crack, they'd always know to come to Corey or Carson. We wouldn't rip them off, we were always very fair with our prices, and usually had them back out in the wild within an hour. After friends of friends began coming to us, we realized our potential. We decided to start a business to handle cell phone repairs, laptop glass repairs, computer virus removal, and a variety of other services to help 'revive' 'tech' –– hence, TechRevival.
