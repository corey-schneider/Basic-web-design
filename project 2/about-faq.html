Frequently Asked Questions


Q: How long does a phone screen repair take?
A: If parts are available and the phone was dropped off before 4pm, it should be done on the same business day.

Q: How much will my repair cost?
A: Check out our Estimate / Quote section in the toolbar above.

Q: Do you do mail-in repairs?
A: At the moment we currently do not accept mail-in orders due to USPS / FedEx delivery issues.

Q: Can you unlock iCloud locked devices?
A: Without the iCloud email and password associated with the device, nobody can unlock it -- not even Apple.

Q: My device was water damaged, but it's been more than 24 hours. Can it still be repaired?
A: No. Corrosion has already gotten too deep into the phone.

Q: What are your business hours?
A: Our business hours are listed under the About page in the toolbar above.
